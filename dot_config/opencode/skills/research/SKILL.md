# Research Workflow

- Define the question, decision, scope, and freshness requirements before
  collecting sources.
- Prefer primary sources where practical. Triangulate material claims and seek
  conflicting or disconfirming evidence.
- Distinguish established facts, inference, speculation, and unknowns.
- Record source provenance and limitations when they materially affect the
  conclusion.
- Stop when additional sources no longer change the material conclusion.
